As a faculty member at the Universidad Autónoma del Estado de México, I have dedicated more than two decades to promoting Open Access in academic publishing. Holding a PhD in Social Sciences, my academic career encompasses a diverse range of disciplines such as Sociology, Regional and Urban Studies, Social Theory, Education and Data Science and Analytics.

In addition, I'm senior consultant at Scientificomm. I have collaborated in the development of current methods of evaluation of academic publications, such as the current law for Open Access in México, approved by the Mexican federal government in May of 2014.

  • 2024-2025: Board of Directors, Open Education Conference, USA.
  • 2024 to Present: Advisory Group Member, OA Switchboard for Global Open Access.
  • 2024 to Present: Programme Committee Member, Born-Digital Collections, Archives and Memory Conference, UK.
  • 2023 to Present: Chair of Red Iberoamericana de Investigación sobre Globalización y Territorio (RII).
  • 2023 to Present: Scientific Committee Member Red Latinoamericana de Sistemas Sociales y Complejidad (RELASSC).
